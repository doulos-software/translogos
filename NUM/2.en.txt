And Jehovah speaketh unto Moses
`Each by his standard
And those encamping eastward towards the sun-rising
and his host
And those encamping by him `are of' the tribe of Issachar; and the prince of the sons of Issachar `is' Nethaneel son of Zuar;"
and his host
The tribe of Zebulun; and the prince of the sons of Zebulun `is' Eliab son of Helon;"
and his host
all those numbered of the camp of Judah `are' a hundred thousand
The standard of the camp of Reuben `is' southward
and his host
And those encamping by him `are of' the tribe of Simeon; and the prince of the sons of Simeon `is' Shelumiel son of Zurishaddai;"
and his host
And the tribe of Gad; and the prince of the sons of Gad `is' Eliasaph son of Reuel;"
and his host
All those numbered of the camp of Reuben `are' a hundred thousand
And the tent of meeting -- the camp of the Levites -- hath journeyed in the midst of the camps; as they encamp so they journey
The standard of the camp of Ephraim
and his host
And by him `is' the tribe of Manasseh; and the prince of the sons of Manasseh `is' Gamaliel son of Pedahzur;"
and his host
And the tribe of Benjamin; and the prince of the sons of Benjamin `is' Abidan son of Gideoni;"
and his host
All those numbered of the camp of Ephraim `are' a hundred thousand
The standard of the camp of Dan `is' northward
and his host
And those encamping by him `are of' the tribe of Asher; and the prince of the sons of Asher `is' Pagiel son of Ocran;"
and his host
And the tribe of Naphtali; and the prince of the sons of Naphtali `is' Ahira son of Enan;"
and his host
All those numbered of the camp of Dan `are' a hundred thousand
These `are' those numbered of the sons of Israel by the house of their fathers; all those numbered of the camps by their hosts `are' six hundred thousand
And the Levites have not numbered themselves in the midst of the sons of Israel
And the sons of Israel do according to all that Jehovah hath commanded Moses; so they have encamped by their standards
