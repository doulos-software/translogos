A Psalm of Asaph. O God
They gave the dead bodies of Thy servants Food for the fowls of the heavens
They have shed their blood As water round about Jerusalem
We have been a reproach to our neighbours
Till when
Pour Thy fury on the nations who have not known Thee
For `one' hath devoured Jacob
Remember not for us the iniquities of forefathers
Help us
Why do the nations say
Let the groaning of the prisoner come in before Thee
And turn Thou back to our neighbours
And we
